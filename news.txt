Latest World News Headlines - June 16, 2026

### **Top International News**
*   **US-Iran Peace Deal:** President Donald Trump is promoting a preliminary peace deal with Iran aimed at ending the conflict and reopening the Strait of Hormuz. While Trump indicated that oil shipping through the strait is beginning to resume, the full text of the memorandum of understanding remains unpublished, creating uncertainty among G7 allies regarding specific terms such as nuclear inspections and sanctions relief.
*   **Israel's Reaction:** The peace deal has drawn a mixed and critical reaction from Israeli officials. Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu stated that Israel would prioritize its own security interests, emphasizing that Iran will not be permitted to possess a nuclear weapon regardless of the agreement.
*   **US Military Tragedy:** Eight people are believed to have died following the crash of a US Air Force B-52 Stratofortress bomber in California. The aircraft went down during a "routine test mission" shortly after takeoff from Edwards Air Force Base.
*   **UK Social Media Ban:** British Prime Minister Keir Starmer announced a government policy to ban all under-16s from accessing major social media applications, citing the need to prioritize child safety.
*   **EU Sanctions on Russia:** The European Union has adopted a new package of sanctions targeting Russia’s "shadow fleet," energy revenues, and military-industrial complex in response to the ongoing war in Ukraine.
